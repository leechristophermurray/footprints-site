KJV Bible Study

Fact-checking, related topics, and study suggestions powered by on-device AI — your data never leaves your phone.

📝

Quizzes

Test your knowledge with quizzes generated from your study progress. Multiple modes keep things fresh and challenging.

Cross-Device Sync

Study on your phone, tablet, or Chromebook. End-to-end encrypted sync through your own Google Drive — no servers, no middlemen.

Listen to Scripture

Two ways to hear the Word: verse-by-verse with your device's voice, or chapter-by-chapter as natural narration streamed from the cloud. Cached for offline once you've heard it.

⌚

Wear, Watch, Drive

A daily-verse complication and tile on Wear OS. A reader on Android TV with Cast from your phone. Voice-first scripture playback in Android Auto and AAOS-equipped vehicles.

🌗

Footprints Digital

A clean digital watch face for Wear OS — large gold clock, date row, four cardinal complications. Distributed as a separate listing on Google Play.

Built on principles

Footprints is designed with conviction: your spiritual life is deeply personal, and your data should stay that way. Every feature is built privacy-first, with on-device processing and zero-knowledge encryption.

There is no premium tier. Every user gets the full Footprints experience. The app is sustained by optional weekly contributions or a single rewarded ad — your choice.
